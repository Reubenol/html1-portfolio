Top films of all time according to Reuben

Ranking of top five

1. Lord Of The Rings: The Return Of The King

The best film ever made, it has everything.

2. Gladiator

Hero story, amazing soundtrack.

3. The Imitation Game

Inspiring patriotic movie and sad.

4. Good Will Hunting

Genius who learns to forgive himself, amazing acting.

5. Patch Adams

Uplifting film, shows the power of humour and positivity.
